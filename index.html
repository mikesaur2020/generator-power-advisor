Help

What is the Live Fuel Tracker?
The primary tab. It tells you how long you can run on your current fuel, which fuel is active, and whether you'll make it through the night.
What is Fuel Burn Reference?
Static tables showing estimated burn rates at different load levels. Use it for trip planning — not for live tracking.
How does Auto Fuel Selection work?
LPG hose connected: Generator uses propane. Gasoline is not consumed.
LPG hose disconnected: Generator starts and runs on gasoline.
Propane runs out: Generator shuts down — it will not auto-switch to gasoline. Disconnect the LPG hose and restart to run on gasoline.
Running on gasoline + connect propane hose: Generator automatically switches to propane.
Why switch A/C to Fan Only?
A/C Cooling draws ~1,700W. Microwave/toaster/coffee maker/hair dryer each draw 1,000–1,500W. Running both at once exceeds the generator's capacity. Switching A/C to Fan Only (250W) frees up ~1,450W for the high-load appliance.
How is Overnight Confidence calculated?
Based on estimated runtime at the current load. High ✅ = 10+ hrs, Moderate ⚠️ = 6–10 hrs, Low ❌ = less than 6 hrs. Two values are shown: Propane Only (conservative) and Combined (if you manually switch to gasoline after propane).
What is Combined Runtime?
Propane runtime + gasoline reserve runtime. Only achievable if you manually disconnect the LPG hose after propane runs out, then restart the generator.
What does the Calculator tab do?
Estimates the generator load from which appliances are on, and gives a Safe / Near Capacity / Unsafe verdict with what to change. Loads are typical estimates — verify against your real-world results.
What is the 30A Shore Power tab?
A separate check for when you plug into a campground 30A pedestal instead of running the generator. It converts your selected appliance load to amps (Watts ÷ 120) and tells you if it's Safe (≤24A), Near Limit (24–30A), or Likely to Trip the Breaker (>30A). A 30A service is 3,600W max, with 2,880W (24A) as the recommended continuous limit. No fuel, runtime, or elevation logic applies.
